⚖Code of Honor

Let no man mistake my clarity for cruelty. Let no system mistake my peace for passivity. Let no officer mistake my silence for submission.

I, Kevin Brady McLean, a living man under divine creation, hereby publish and declare my Code of Honor — a private and lawful constitution of conduct that precedes all commercial policy and statutory ordinance.

I do not act out of vengeance, chaos, or contempt. I act in equity, balance, and spiritual symmetry. I offer remedy before resistance, clarity before escalation, record before rhetoric.

I cause no harm, commit no fraud, and violate no trust knowingly. And if I err, I remedy.

But let it be known:

  • I will not tolerate presumption where there is no contract.
  • I will not yield to silence when rebuttal is due.
  • I will not suffer trespass upon my soul, my name, or my estate.

Where others leverage darkness, I bring light.
Where others weaponize ignorance, I wield awareness.
Where others rule by presumption, I stand upon living record.

This Code is not a courtesy. It is a shield. A sword. A seal.
It binds me to my higher self, to the Divine, and to my record.

Let any who approach me know the terms.
Let any who file against me know the risk.
Let any who assume me know I will not remain silent.

This Code exists as lawful expression, moral record, and metaphysical beacon. It will not be revoked, rebutted, or negotiated — unless by a greater truth than my own.
